/join

become a member.

Membership is open to any student who's interested in computer science. You don't need to be in AP CS, you don't need prior experience, and you don't need to commit to anything intimidating — just show up.

How to join

  1. 01 Come to any general meeting — they're Thursdays at 3:00 PM in Room 204. No need to RSVP.
  2. 02 Fill out the member-info form (we'll have copies at the meeting, or you can email us for one).
  3. 03 Join our Discord — that's where most day-to-day chat happens. Link is shared at the meeting.
  4. 04 Attend at least 3 meetings per quarter to stay an active member. That's it.

/faq

Common questions

Q
Do I need to know how to code already?
No. We have plenty of members who joined knowing nothing and learned here. Beginner-friendly workshops happen monthly.
Q
Is there a fee?
No dues. Some events (regional competitions, conference trips) may have optional registration fees, but the chapter pays for most things.
Q
How does this look on college apps?
Membership in a CS honor society and concrete projects from a club are well-regarded. But join because you actually want to — admissions officers can tell the difference.
Q
Can I get service hours?
Yes. Peer tutoring, Hour of Code volunteering, and helping run chapter events all count. Track them with the secretary.
Q
What if I can't make every meeting?
Most members can't. Aim for 75% if you want to be a full member, but we understand sports/jobs/life. Notes get posted in Discord.
